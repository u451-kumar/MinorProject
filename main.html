Rivista Science Publisher


Rivista Science Publisher (RSP) aimed to deliver the information to the researcher, Scientists, teachers and young professionals of modern era about Science & Technology. Researchers from the universities, technical and non technical institutions across the world can access articles from Rivista Science Publisher which is fully open access. It provides the platform to the young researchers to present their ideas about current progress in various sectors of research and get the access to the work of others around the world. Rivista Science Publisher is a wholly owned profit unit of Scientia Praecepta: Exploring the world of science. The mission of RSP is to support the liberal, technical and enlightening purposes of Scientia Praecepta through scholarly open access publishing in the fields of the Science & technology on its own behalf. RSP Publishing's portfolio comprises of highly admired new journals of current era in the field of Physical Science, Chemical Science and Engineering technology such as Achievement in material science &Technology (AMST), Achievement in Polymer science (APS), Achievement in nano-science & Technology (ANST), Achievement in magnetic material (AMM), Achievement in condensed matter physics (ACMP), Achievement in material modification by radiation (AMMR), Achievement in Computer science & Information Technology (ACSIT), Achievement in mechanical & automation Technology (AMAT), Achievement in electrical & communication Technology (AECT), Achievement in material science &Technology (AMST), Achievement in electrical & instrumentation Technology (AEIT) Achievement of Material Science & Technology (AMST) is the first open access journal of Scientia Praecepta which consists of various current research topics such as solar cells, thin films, graphene, Magnetic materials and nano-particles and many more. AMST is aimed to incorporate Editors and Reviewers who are the leaders in publishing innovative research in the pinnacle areas of Materials Science and Technology. Scientia Praecepta


The nucleus of Scientia Praecepta: Exploring the world of Science is to allocate the novel ideas and current trends in Material Science, Engineering Science & Technology. Papers in related areas are considered for the specified Journal, if they contain information of direct significance to Journal point of view. Our purpose is to compensate for the increasing specialization of sciences.

Scientia Praecepta accepts:

  • Original full research papers
  • Review articles (should cover a part of the subject of active current interest only)
  • Short Communications

Scientia Praecepta offers:
Peer reviewed Open Access journals Only original research papers are acceptable that should be of current international interest. The material should not have been previously published elsewhere, except in a preliminary form. All rights are reserved to the editor to finalize the work of interest for the journal. Short Communications should be as completely documented, both by reference to the literature and description of the experimental procedures employed, as a regular paper. They should not occupy more than 4 printed pages (about 8 manuscript pages, including figures, etc.). Scientia Praecepta is the entity of Rivista science publisher, a series of scientific and research journal in the field of Science, Engineering and technology that is the growing publisher of scientific and technical content in modern era. Today, Scientia Praecepta is currently achieving the goal to connect with educational and technical scientist and researcher in the physical science, Natural science, Engineering and technology in government as well as private research organizations

Open Access


Open Access is a publishing model, without requiring any subscription to the journal that provides direct, worldwide, barrier-free access to the full text of research articles. To display the work publically in digital medium the authors are permitted to use, allocate and convey the main purpose of the related work, subjected to proper authorship. In this model, the cost of publication is covered by the author’s institution or research funds (as per the norms of publisher). The publisher is liable to publish the material to all interested readers who can access the journal at the cost of open access charges without paying any subscription fee. Other than their cost-recovery model, Open Access journals are no different from traditional subscription-based journals; they undergo the same peer-review and quality control as any other scholarly journal. Moreover, Open Access allows for maximum visibility, uptake and use of the published material.
