ADTU JOURNALS


The phrase Scientia Praecepta is Latin aphorism meaning “Knowledge with principles”. Scientia Praecepta is a series of research Journals where we connect teachers, scientists, educators and researchers through worldwide from government as well as private sectors. We provide high-quality research papers, engaging, and relevant information for our immense spectators. Scientia Praecepta helps researcher to communicate their findings beyond their specialty through worldwide. It offers the research community significant visibility and accessibility for whole community to take an interest in science research and technology.
